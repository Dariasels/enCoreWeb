Short introduction:

If you're reading this you probably know who I am.

I am that random girl who never stops thinking about leaving Belgium :) (That probably explains why you met me in a different country:)

his website is an accumulation of many of my passions, inspirations, and plans.It's both a technical project to me (to learn to code, host, and manage a website), as well as a life-long personal project to organize my travel ideas and share them with friends.

I intend to live life the fullest with the poeple I care about, that drive me and that want to explore the world together.

Here you'll find different types of trips that I want to organize, share, and experience with you. Each trip page contains more information about the trip type, and allows you to sign up so that you can stay up to date with the latest plans.

In either case, once you sign up, you will be notified about a new event. If you see anything that you like, please hit the 'I'm in' button and get notified about your event. Then, let's embrark on this adventure together!

This project is constantly evolving, so feel free to give me feeback at anytime. Also, if you have any trip ideas, please post them on the 'Suggestions' page. And, if you have tips about a certain topic (like 'whats the best running gel', 'in which month should you visit the salt lakes in bolivia', or ' how do I train for a marathon'), please share them on the 'Inspiration' page, so that I can put them online.

There's only one rule, be part of the community once you sign up, and let's make core memories together!
