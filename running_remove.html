Running Trips

The goal is to run (half) marathons around the world with people we meet on the way. I want to build a community of extrordinary people who motivate eachother to work towards the same physical and mental goals. All of this while meeting eachother in astonishing places all around the world. Sidenote: votes are used to assess popularity --> more votes = higher chance of being organized. (So, only vote for those you can actually attend)
